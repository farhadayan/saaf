SAVE ENVIRONMENT - Jute is the strongest of all-natural fibers and considered as fiber of the future. Bangladesh is the leading producers of jute plant. The fibre is extracted from jute plant after natural processing (fibre extraction). It is a biodegradable and eco-friendly fibre. It has many advantages over synthetics and protects the environment and maintains the ecological balance. This 100% biodegradable natural material is made from the bark of the jute plant.Jute plants are also a Carbon sink. During 120 days of jute-growing period one hectare of jute plants can absorb about 15 MT of CO2 from atmosphere and liberate 11 MT of O2 . Thus, jute has gained an advantage as an eco-friendly alternative and is therefore an ideal packaging solution for many different products. Jute is also used for making promotional bags and lifestyles items.
GIVE EDUCATION TO CHILDREN - More than 60 million children live in Bangladesh. Half of them grow up in the most abject poverty. Vast amount children are not getting education for poverty. We are working on educational support to Bangladeshi children who are out from education for poverty. We believe education can change economic situation and voice of a society. We are in touch with three orphanage, initially we have started with one orphanage where 23 parentless children stay and 24 more children come for study. We help that orphanage to continue their great job.
WOMEN EMPOWERMENT - Economic empowerment is the capacity of women to participate in, contribute to and benefit from growth processes in ways which recognise the value of their contributions, respect their dignity and make it possible to negotiate a fairer distribution of the benefits of growth. When you buy a bag it helps women worker to get their identity and recognition as equal contributor to the soceity. They don't want to be in a cage, basically you are helping them to get their freedom and identity.
