JUTE PRODUCTS help in decreasing environmental pollution. It is biodegradable and 100% compostable. Jute bags are extremely strong, reusable and environmentally friendly
SAVE ENVIRONMENT thanks to greater awareness of environmental concerns In terms of reusability, sustainability, and biodegradability, this highly cultivable jute plant fibre can potentially be the solution for a lot of environmental threats
NO PAPER BAG The life cycle of paper is damaging to the environment from beginning to end. It starts off with a tree being cut down and ends its life by being burned – emitting carbon dioxide in the atmosphere.
NO PLASTIC BAG Yearly, trillions of plastic bags are polluting our environment and ocean. Even biodegradable plastic has many disadvantages such as it needs costly equipment for recycling and it absorbs heat faster, also it does not solve Ocean pollution problems.
BUY MY EDUCATION We believe knowledge is power. We spend 10% of profit to those children in Bangladesh for their education. Your contribution to buy a bag is not only helping environment but education for children in Bangladesh.
WOMAN EMPOWERMENT When you buy a bag it helps women worker to get their identity and recognition as equal contributor to the soceity. They don't want to be in a cage, basically you are helping them to get their freedom and identity.
